Floating glass notification with a liquid-bubble status marker; springs in. Render into a fixed-position stack in real apps.

```jsx
<Toast tone="success" title="Project shipped" message="Aero Mail is live." onClose={dismiss} />
<Toast tone="danger" message="Upload failed — retry?" />
```

Tones: `accent` · `success` · `warning` · `danger`. `title` + `message`; `onClose` for dismiss.
